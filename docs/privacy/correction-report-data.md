# Correction-report data

Reporter name and email are optional. They are restricted by `correction.view_contact`, excluded
from general logs and public responses, and should be redacted after 12 months. Bounded descriptions
are retained for operational resolution for no more than 24 months by default. Non-identifying
status, task linkage, resolution, and audit history may be retained for accountability.

Users are warned not to submit medical, legal, immigration, abuse, crisis, credential, government
identifier, or other sensitive personal information. Self-hosters must publish their operator,
retention schedule, contact method, and applicable privacy rights before public use.
