# ADR 0010: Accountable AI-assisted contributions
Status: Accepted
## Context
Coding agents are useful but may fabricate or introduce insecure, unlicensed output.
## Decision
Allow material AI assistance with disclosure, understanding, testing, and human responsibility.
## Alternatives considered
Ban AI; permit undisclosed use.
## Consequences
Tools remain available without shame; reviewers receive context and may reject unsupported work.
