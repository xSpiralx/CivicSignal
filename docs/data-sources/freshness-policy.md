# 2025–2026 freshness policy

Every candidate must carry a trustworthy record, dataset-release, dataset-update, or provider-verification date in 2025 or 2026. Retrieval time is stored separately and never substitutes for freshness. Missing or older dates are rejected into the batch error summary.

The four independent timestamps are source-record update, dataset update, retrieval, and CivicSignal review. A current catalog page does not make demonstrably old underlying records current.
