# Code of Conduct

CivicSignal follows the [Contributor Covenant, version 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/). Contributors must help maintain a welcoming, harassment-free environment regardless of identity or experience. Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, threats, sexualized conduct, trolling, publishing private information, or conduct inappropriate for a public-interest community.

Report conduct concerns privately through the maintainer contact channel described in `SECURITY.md`; do not include crisis information. Maintainers may correct, warn, temporarily restrict, or permanently remove participation. Enforcement decisions should protect affected people, preserve confidentiality where possible, and document conflicts of interest.
